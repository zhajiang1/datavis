For UK, the pattern is quite similar to what we saw in US: case fatality rate is around 0.01-0.03 most of the time (in early 2020 it reaches 0.2 limit as low confirmed cases). There's a peak confirmed near the end of 2021 due to Omicron[1]. And then the case fatality rate drops to 0.001-0.004. Similarly, UK government also withdrawn the law to wear face covering in public spaces[2].
